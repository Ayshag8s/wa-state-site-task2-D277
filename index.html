5 Facts About the State

  1. The state of Washington is home to up to 7.8 million people.
  2. Washington was incorporated to the United States in 1889.
  3. Washington state makes up the majority of the Pacific Northwest reigon of The United States.
  4. Washington scales generously between urban centers and vast rural landscapes.
  5. The average income across the state is roughly between $50,000 to $80,000 per year.
